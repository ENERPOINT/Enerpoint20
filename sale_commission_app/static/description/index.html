Odoo Sales Commissions Based on Target with Amount by Sales order , Invoice and Payment Apps

Sale order commission based on target amount Odoo apps helps user to manage Commission by Amount Range with fixed amount or percentage amount in Sales/Invoice/Payment. Commission applied for sale confirmation, invoice validate and payment registration process, user can select choice from the configuration options .

Sales Commission on specific Product, Product Category and Sales Team .

User can setup a commission for a product, product category and sales team by fixed amount or fixed percentage.

Commission on confirmation of order.

Commission will be applied on sale order confirmation, invoice validation and payment registration.

Vendor bill and Report for the Commission Report.

Vendor Bill will be created when user click on create invoice and report can printed for selected records.

Description

Configuration for Commission

User can configure commission on Sale confirmation, Invoice Payment and Customer Payment, and calculation on base of product, product category and sales team.


Commission Configure for Product

User can configure commission for product by fixed amount and by percentage.



Commission Configure for Product Category

User can configure commission for product category by fixed amount and by percentage.



Commission Configure for Sales Team

User can configure commission for Sales Team by fixed amount and by percentage.



Sale Order Confirm

When user click on confirm order, sale commission line will be created for that sale order.



Invoice Confirm

When user click on validate, sale commission line will be created for that Invoice.



Customer Payment

When user click on Register payment, sale commission line will be created for that Payment.



Sale Commission Records

Sale Commission record will be created on the base of customer and product. On click of create invoice, Vendor Bill will be created.


Sale Commission Report

User can print report for selected record.
